Questions operators ask us

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need to switch my current PMS?
No. Vesta OS connects to your existing channels directly (Airbnb, Booking, Expedia, Vrbo) and can run alongside your PMS during migration. Most operators start with guest comms and pricing, then move offline operations to Cleanfy.
What happens when VRM isn't sure how to reply?
Any message with negative sentiment, refund risk or compliance implications is auto-routed to your team's priority queue. You can also set "review every reply" mode while you're getting comfortable.
Can I bring my own vendors into Cleanfy?
Yes. Onboarding a third-party cleaning company, maintenance team or inventory vendor into Cleanfy takes minutes. You can also tap into the Vesta OS city partner network where coverage exists.
How does the 5% revenue share on pricing work?
It's 5% of the net revenue from listings that use Autonomous Pricing, charged monthly. First month is free. You can set rate floors, ceilings, and minimum LOS rules; we optimize inside them.
Is there a minimum number of listings?
No hard minimum. Vesta OS is built for operators with 5–50 units, but we also serve larger portfolios. Contact sales for multi-city deployments.
Who owns the data and the listings?
You do. Your channels, your content, your guest data. Vesta OS is your operational layer — you can export at any time, and we never share data across customers.
