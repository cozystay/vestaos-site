FAQ

Common questions.

Can I use just one module?
Yes. Each module is independent. Most operators start with Autonomous Pricing for the fastest ROI, then add Guest Comms and Cleanfy as they scale.
How does billing work?
Guest Comms is billed monthly per listing. Autonomous Pricing is billed as a percentage of each booking processed. Ops Autopilot is billed per job dispatched. No setup fees, no long-term contracts.
Is there a minimum portfolio size?
No minimum. We work with operators from 5 listings to 500+. Pricing stays the same regardless of portfolio size.
What happens when humans need to intervene?
C Claw routes high-risk escalations to the owner with full context. Cleanfy flags jobs that fail photo QA. You only see what genuinely needs your attention — typically less than 10% of tasks.
How quickly can I get started?
Onboarding takes less than 24 hours. Book a 30-minute demo and we'll connect a sample listing and run all three modules live — no migration headaches.
