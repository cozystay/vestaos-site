Our mission

Make every operator run like a 10× operator — without a 10× team.

We started as a property management company. We felt every bottleneck, every missed SLA, every 2-AM dispatch call. Then LLMs arrived — and we rebuilt everything we'd learned into an AI-native platform.

Our story

From 100 rooms to 1,012 — and now a platform.

2022. We launched Cozystay as a boutique property manager with 100 rooms. We did it the way the industry always did: spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and a lot of caffeine.

2023. 300 rooms. 15 FTEs. We built internal tools — a pricing optimizer, a length-of-stay model — but humans still had to execute every reply, every dispatch, every QA. Growth meant hiring.

2024. 715 rooms. 35 FTEs. The math was brutal: ~20 listings per person, a 20-minute average reply time against a 3-minute industry SLA, and 2-hour pricing cycles twice a day. Margins were compressing as we scaled.

By 2025, we were managing 1,012 rooms. LLMs had crossed the reliability threshold. We realized for the first time that not just the decisions — but the execution — could be automated. We started building VRM and Cleanfy in parallel, on top of 10M+ of our own operational data points.

March 2026. VRM launched. The operator flywheel became an AI Native operating platform — and we opened it up to every operator who wants to stop scaling their payroll.
