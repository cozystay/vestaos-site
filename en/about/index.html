Our story

Built by operators,
for operators.

We didn't study the alternative accommodation industry. We ran it — at scale, across markets, for years. VestaOS is the operating system we wished we had.

The problem we lived.

Before we built VestaOS, we operated short-term rental listings at scale across Vancouver and Los Angeles. Every new listing meant more headcount. Guest messages piled up. Pricing took two analysts working full days. Cleaners were dispatched by group chat. Quality verification was manual and lagged by days.

We built internal tools to survive. Then we realized: every operator in this industry has the same problem. The bottleneck isn't capital or inventory — it's operations. And operations doesn't scale linearly unless you automate it.

So we took everything we'd learned — our SOPs, our escalation paths, our exception-handling logic, our 10M+ data points from real operations — and built the OS we wished we had. That's VestaOS.

Why we're different.

Every AI tool in this space is built by engineers who studied the industry. We're the only team that actually ran the business first — and then automated it.

That means our models aren't trained on generic hospitality data. They're trained on our own escalations, our own SOPs, our own exception paths. The edge cases aren't edge cases to us — they're patterns we've already solved.

Our mission.

To make every alternative accommodation listing an autonomous P&L — where the technology handles the operations, and the operator focuses on growth.

We're headquartered in Richmond, BC, Canada. We're an Official Airbnb and Booking.com Connectivity Partner.

How we work

Principles that guide us.

Operator-first

Every feature starts with a real operational problem. We don't build for demos — we build for the 3am edge case that costs operators money.

Trust by design

Automation without control is a liability. Every module has human-in-the-loop options. Operators choose their trust level — from full audit to full autopilot.

Measurable outcomes

We don't sell features. We sell outcomes — response time, SLA rate, revenue lift, labor reduction. Every claim we make is backed by operator data.

Compounding learning

Every dispatch, photo, and reply feeds back into the platform. The more operators use it, the better it gets — for everyone.
