How Vesta OS Works

Vesta OS connects guest communication, pricing, dispatch, QA, and settlement into one AI-native operating loop.

1. Connect your channels and listings

Vesta OS starts by connecting the systems that already run your accommodation business: booking channels, listings, guest messages, pricing inputs, task workflows, and operational records.

The goal is to give your team one operational layer instead of scattered tools, chats, spreadsheets, and manual follow-ups.

2. Centralize operational context

Reservations, guest messages, listing details, cleaning tasks, vendor updates, QA records, and settlement information are brought into structured workflows.

This gives AI and operators the context needed to make faster and more consistent decisions.

3. Let AI handle routine work

Vesta OS can classify guest messages, draft replies, support approved auto-responses, monitor pricing signals, create operational tasks, and route work to the right team or vendor.

Routine work becomes automated, while sensitive or high-risk cases can still be reviewed by humans.

4. Dispatch and verify offline execution

For cleaning, inspection, maintenance, and field tasks, Vesta OS helps assign work, track status, collect photo evidence, verify completion, and flag exceptions.

Online decisions and offline execution are connected in the same operating system.

5. Review exceptions and keep control

Operators can define review rules, escalation triggers, approval requirements, and human-in-the-loop controls.

This allows teams to automate repetitive work without losing visibility or accountability.

6. Close the loop with reporting and settlement

Completed tasks, QA records, pricing actions, guest communication, and operational exceptions can feed into reporting, settlement review, and owner-facing transparency.

Every workflow becomes part of a measurable operating record.

The operating loop

Connect data → understand context → automate routine work → escalate exceptions → verify execution → report outcomes.

That is how Vesta OS helps operators scale without scaling headcount linearly.
