Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy explains what is not allowed when using Vesta OS. It applies to users, customers, administrators, vendors, and anyone accessing the Services through your account or organization.

No Illegal or Harmful Use

You may not use Vesta OS to violate applicable law, regulation, contract, platform rule, or third-party right. You may not use the Services to support fraud, deception, harassment, threats, unsafe activity, or other harmful conduct.

No Misuse of Personal Data

You may not use Vesta OS to collect, process, share, or disclose personal information without proper authority, consent, or legal basis.

No Security Abuse

You may not access systems without authorization, bypass security controls, test vulnerabilities without permission, upload malicious code, or interfere with the integrity, availability, or performance of the Services.

No Platform Abuse

You may not reverse engineer, scrape, copy, resell, overload, manipulate, or commercially exploit the Services except as permitted by agreement.

Responsible Use of AI

You may not use AI features to generate, approve, or send content that is unlawful, deceptive, abusive, discriminatory, unsafe, or misleading. You are responsible for reviewing AI outputs where appropriate.

Operational Safety

You may not use the Services in a way that creates unreasonable safety risks for guests, staff, vendors, homeowners, or the public.

Third-Party Platforms

When using Vesta OS with third-party platforms, you must comply with the terms, policies, and technical restrictions of those platforms.

Enforcement

If we believe this Policy has been violated, we may investigate, suspend access, remove content, restrict features, terminate accounts, or take other appropriate action.

Contact

To report abuse or ask questions about this Policy, please contact us through the contact information provided on our website.

This page is provided for general information and does not constitute legal advice.
