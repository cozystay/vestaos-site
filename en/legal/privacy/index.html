Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Vesta OS collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use our websites, applications, software, AI-powered workflows, and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information, business and operational information, guest and end-user information, device and usage information, billing information, and information provided through integrations.

How We Use Information

AI Processing

Vesta OS may process operational data through AI models and automated systems to classify messages, generate draft replies, support pricing decisions, detect exceptions, verify task completion, and improve workflow efficiency.

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain business records.

Security

We use reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure.

International Transfers

Vesta OS may process information in countries or regions other than where it was originally collected. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

We may use cookies and similar technologies to operate our website, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and improve user experience.
